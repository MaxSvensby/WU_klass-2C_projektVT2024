How to rotate

Deadside rotations

In Fortnite, "deadside rotations" refer to strategic movements toward areas with fewer opponents. Players utilize this tactic to avoid confrontation, gather resources, and position themselves advantageously for end-game scenarios. Deadside rotations demand careful planning and awareness of the game's dynamic map, contributing to players' survival and success.

Straight line rotations

In Fortnite, "straight line rotations" entail moving directly from one point to another without deviating from the most efficient path. This strategy minimizes exposure to enemy fire and optimizes resource acquisition, especially during transitions between safe zones. Straight line rotations demand swift decision-making and map awareness, enabling players to navigate terrain obstacles and potential threats while conserving valuable time and resources for crucial engagements.
